FORM EMPLOYMENT AGENCIES CAREER GUIDANCE REPORTING

The Office of Career Services is the department/unit that coordinates career guidance programmes in employment agencies. The role of the Career Guidance Contact Person (CGCP) in the Office of Career Services (OCS) is to monitor and report on the progress made in regard to the establishment and operationalization of the Office of Career Services. To enable the CGCP of the OCS to report on this progress, the State Department for Labour and Skills Development has automated the reporting form (see below). Please use this form to fill in the quarterly and annual progress reports for your agency.
