CAREER GUIDANCE EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS REPORTING FORM

The Office of Career Services is the department/unit that coordinates career guidance programmes in education and training institutions. The role of the Career Guidance Focal Point (CGFP) in the Office of Career Services (OCS) is to monitor and report on the progress made in regard to the establishment and operationalization of the Office of Career Services. To enable the CGFP of the OCS to report on this progress, the State Department for Labour and Skills Development has automated the reporting form (see below). Please use this form to fill in the quarterly and annual progress reports for your institution.
