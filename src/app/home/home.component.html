Value Proposition Sections

Skills Training & Mentorship

The program aims to provide comprehensive training and mentorship, emphasizing online work readiness and access to the Gig Economy. Participants can benefit from practical skills development and guidance to secure meaningful opportunities.

National Online Employment Skill Development Programme (NOESDP)

NOESDP offers a structured approach to skill development for employment, focusing on enhancing job skills and readying participants for the evolving job market. The program provides the opportunity to acquire in-demand skills for competitive advantages in the job market.

National Tech-Industry Data (NTID)

NTID aims to provide a platform for young innovators to access industry contacts and work-based learning opportunities. It offers insights into the tech industry and facilitates networking with potential employers, enhancing the prospects of career development and real-world application skills.
