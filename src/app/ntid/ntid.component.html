NATIONAL TECH-INDUSTRY DATA FOR YOUNG INNOVATORS VOLUNTEERSHIP PROGRAMME

The National Tech-Industry Database (NTID) falls under the State Department for Labour and Skills Development in the Ministry of Labour and Social Protection. The NTID is a data management solution which collects data and information from technology based industries to facilitate the development of policies, strategies and programmes on tech-work based learning programmes that target young innovators in Kenya.
