NATIONAL ONLINE WORK READINESS SKILLS TRAINING AND MENTORSHIP PROGRAMME

Dear Applicant,

Thank you for expressing interest in the national online work readiness skills training & mentorship programme, which is delivered in partnership with State Department for ICT & Digital Economy - Ajira Digital Program. The objective of the programme is to equip the young people with the requisite knowledge, skills, and attitudes for digital/online work to access decent work and earn an income from the Gig & Freelancing Economy.

The training will be virtually conducted for 5 Days, from 4th to 8th March 2024 at 8.00AM - 4.30 PM and will cover Cross-cutting, Basic & Advanced Modules of the Ajira Digital curriculum. The Basic modules to be covered are Digital Marketing, Data Entry and Virtual Assistant. The Advanced Modules to be covered are AI for Graphic Design. The cross-cutting modules to be covered will include; Introduction to Digital & Digitally Enabled work, Personal Digital Profile Creation, Online Work Safety & Data Protection, and Soft Skills. The training will be followed by a 4-Weeks flexible mentorship program which will entail 2 virtual sessions per week each on a 2 -hour duration & chat-based mentorship. At the end of the mentorship program, there shall be a Job Linkages webinar & a graduation thereafter. (Note that certificates shall be awarded to trainees who fully complete the training and mentorship sessions)

Interested candidates to apply for either Basic or Advanced modules training & mentorship from the list provided below.
